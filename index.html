Biography

Stefan Werner is a lecturer of linguistics and language technology at the University of Eastern Finland in Joensuu (and a “docent” for language technology at the University of Turku). His research interests include vowel acoustics, acoustic assessment of dysarthria and new approaches to make computational sense of speech data. He is in charge of the Erasmus Mundus Masters Program in Clinical Linguistics (EMCL+) at UEF.
